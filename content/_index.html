#OurPhilosophy

For anything to grow, you need three things:

Heat

Heat means work.
Upstream contributions, downstream recipiants. The workers who drive the community.

Light

Light means visibility.
For the projects, contributions, opportunities, challenges, and people that impact the community.

Love

Love means culture and support.
Why we care. How we work. Actions that feed and nurture the community.

Our Mission

GiveGains is focused on funding and nurturing local, grassroots community organizations. These organizations are invaluable to communities, as they are born with compassion and an insider awareness of the people and their true realities. Yet, grassroots organizations are generally critically under-nurtured and under-funded. Routing global resources to local organizations drives us.
